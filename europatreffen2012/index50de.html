Omena Hotels

 

Omena Hotels provide a “value for money”, self- service accommodation right in the heart of the city. The Omena hotel chain has two hotels in Tampere.  Omena Hotel Tampere  1 and Omena Hotel Tampere  2.
.

Facitilities
All of rooms have a double bed and two sofa beds. Towels as well as sheets, quilts and pillows for four people are also provided. There is a wardrobe, a small fridge, a microwave, a kettle (and disposable mugs), a WC, a shower, and a dining table capable of seating four. Every room comes with free internet access. The large-screen TV also acts as an interactive communication channel, providing the latest information about what is happening in town, as well as Pay-per-view films. You can also use it to report any problems to the on-duty staff at the Help Desk. Door codes!
