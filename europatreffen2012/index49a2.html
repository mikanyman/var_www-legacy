Wednesday, June 13th

.

Helsinki, the daughter of the Baltic Sea

.

A whole day trip to the capital of Finland, guided Sightseeing tour by bus incl. a big lunch package and a big special evening soup

From 07:30 a.m. to 11:00 p.m.

68 Euro

 In order to make this highly interesting trip a valuable one, we have arranged for an earlier breakfast for you in our recommended hotels today. Our comfortable busses will pick us up already at 07:30 a.m. at the Scandic Rosendahl and Cumulus Hämeenpuisto Hotel, and at our meeting point at the Keskustori Marketplace in front of the church. All busses go via the Keskustori Marketplace.
.
Driving along nice Finnish roads through the countryside, we will have a short break after about one hour and, will then reach Helsinki at about 10:30 a.m. At the Senate Square, our meeting point, we will then receive our big lunch packages. After a walk of about 250 meters we will come to the famous harbor and the picturesque fish market of Helsinki where our ferries to Suomenlinna (details below) are waiting for us. Besides various delicious Finnish food and fish specialties at the market, you can also find reindeer furs and other hand crafted souvenirs. Guests, who have not booked the Suomenlinna tour, can now enjoy their free time discovering beautiful Helsinki a little on foot until about 03:00 p.m. They may visit the old market hall close by, or stroll through the near Esplanade Park with many possibilities for a snack, a cup of coffee, or look at the many beautiful buildings around the Senate Square.
.

At 02:45 p.m. we will all meet together again at the Senate Square and will begin our guided 2 ½ hours sightseeing tour by bus in Helsinki. Experienced city guides on the busses will take us to all of the famous places, buildings and sites and explain about the history of the capital of Finland. Ending the tour we will visit the impressive Dome Cathedral of Helsinki and together experience unforgettable moments in the dome. Possibilities to buy some extra snacks and beverages exist in many places.
.
At about 06:45 p.m. we will all meet at the Senate Square again and, at 07:00 p.m. drive back in our busses towards Tampere. About another hour later we will take our evening break en route when we will reach the place for our “Big Special Finnish Evening Soup”. After enjoying this specialty in the nature, our busses will then start taking us to Tampere at about 10:00 p.m. And late in the evening, we can still see the countryside of Southern Finland in almost daylight and we will arrive back in Tampere and at our hotels about 11:00 p.m.
.
.

Optional boat trip from the Helsinki harbor to the Suomenlinna Fortress – UNESCO World Heritage Site

(Only in combination with our Helsinki Tour!)

17 Euro

The maximum capacity for the whole trip to Suomenlinna is 240 people. Our ferries will leave the harbor at 10:45 a.m. Also, for Young & Tall people this trip will be an unforgettable one. Suomenlinna is an extraordinary place where you can still see some relicts of the battles about Finland and feel the spirit of the soldiers who came from France, Sweden, Russia and Finland. In 1991, the Suomenlinna fortress was added to the UNESCO World Heritage list as a unique monument of military architecture.
.
After arriving with the ferries about half an hour later in Suomenlinna, we have four groups planned at different times and in different languages for this highly professional video about the history of this huge fortress. Watching the video is optional, but highly recommended. This very interesting video lasts about half an hour for each group and is included in the price. Before or after watching the video in your group, you have time to discover the area. You may eat your lunch packages in “picnic style” on the grass looking over the Baltic Sea and/or purchase some extra food or beverages from a little grocery store.
.
At 02:15 p.m. the ferries will go back to the beautiful harbor of Helsinki where we will walk back to the Senate Square and meet again with the others.
.
.
Dress code:  Casual – use your best and most comfortable walking shoes especially for Suomenlinna. Rain protection (jacket & cap) is recommended. Just in case.
