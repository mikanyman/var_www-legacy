Hostel Sofia

 

A clean, cozy and inexpensive hostel accommodation with good size rooms located close to the city center. Suitable for younger people with little money. Prices are per room!
.
Rooms
Single Room 59 eur / night
Double Room 75 eur / night
Four-bed Room 112 eur / night
Prices include sheets and towels.
Breakfast at extra cost.
.
Bookings
Bookings via email hostelsofia@hostelsofia.fi
or by phone +358 3 2544020
No on-line booking with booking code.
.
Booking code: ET2012FI
Please remember to use our quota’s booking code. Limited quota! Specially negotiated rate at Hostel Sofia. Valid for stays between 9 – 19 June 2012. Book right up to and until 9 May 2012. If cancelled by 18.00 pm day before arrival, an individual customer’s reservation can be cancelled without charge. If the reservation has not been cancelled by according to this condition, the price of one day’s stay will be charged. Payment by cash or credit card at the hotel on arrival.
.
Facitilities
Free wireless internet connection is provided in all rooms. Toilets and showers are in the hallway/corridor. On each floor there is a well-equipped self-catering kitchenette where you can make your own breakfast, supper and prepare other light snacks. You can also choose to take the buffet breakfast  (2011: 6,50€) that is served in the hostel’s cozy little breakfast room from 8 to 9:30 am. Room prices do not include breakfast. Please, order your breakfast at the reception one day before. The lounge downstairs is open from 7:30 am to 10 pm. Sleeping bags are not allowed. Beds 90x200cm without ends.
.
The reception is open Mon-Sat from 8am to 9pm and Sun from 8am to 12pm.
.
Check in after 16PM, check out before 11AM.
If you will be arriving after 9pm or your arrival day is Sunday, please contact reception in advance.
.
